Cave Game

Super Cronos Adventure

by Patrick Gannon and Emily Regöczy

One of the final thing made in Flash before moving onto Unity in uni. In pairs, we were tasked with making a game based on some kind of mythology. This is "loosely" based off the story of Cronos and how he ate Zeus and his siblings, but instead if that he's eating guys running across a bridge.

A and D - To slam with left an right hand

J and L - To grab with left an right hand
